About

The Silence Project is developed on the Unity engine for a collecge project. The game is a puzzle horror game, in which the player is tasked with exploring a mysterious mansion in the hopes of finding a key to unlock the front doors and escape. The player will have to unlock doors to progress through the mansion, and will experience paranormal activities within the house itself, which will take them into hellish locations to retrieve the keys to their escape, or at least get them a step closer to escape.
